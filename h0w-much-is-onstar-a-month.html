How Much is Onstar a Month?

Have you ever found yourself asking, How much is OnStar a month? You're not alone. OnStar's suite of safety, security, and connectivity features has made it an essential service for many GM vehicle owners. However, navigating the various plans and pricing can be a bit overwhelming. Whether you're considering OnStar for the first time or simply looking to understand the monthly costs, this comprehensive guide will provide all the information you need. Let's explore the details and help you find the OnStar plan that best fits your needs and budget!


Understanding the cost of OnStar per month and the details of each plan can help you decide which service best suits your needs. This article will delve into the various OnStar plans, their costs, features, and frequently asked questions, concluding with a summary in tabular form.


OnStar Plans and Pricing

OnStar offers several subscription plans, each with a distinct set of features. The plans can be categorized into Safety & Security, Connected Services, and Add-Ons. Below is a detailed overview of each plan and its monthly cost.


Safety & Security Plans


  1. OnStar Protection Plan
    2. Cost: $19.99 per month
    Features:
    • Automatic Crash Response
    • Emergency Services
    • Crisis Assist
    • Roadside Assistance
    • Stolen Vehicle Assistance

    This plan focuses on providing essential safety and security features, ensuring help is available in emergencies, whether you are involved in an accident or facing a crisis on the road.


  2. OnStar Security Plan
    3. Cost: $24.99 per month
    Features:
    • All features of the Protection Plan
    • Remote Ignition Block
    • Theft Alarm Notification

    The Security Plan builds on the Protection Plan by adding enhanced theft prevention features, offering more comprehensive protection for your vehicle.


Connected Services Plans

  1. OnStar Basic Plan
    2. Cost: Free for five years with new GM vehicles
    Features:
    • Keyfob services (remote start, lock/unlock, horn and lights)
    • Vehicle Diagnostics
    • Dealer Maintenance Notification

    The Basic Plan provides fundamental connectivity features at no additional cost for the first five years, giving new vehicle owners a taste of OnStar’s capabilities.


  2. OnStar Guidance Plan
    3. Cost: $34.99 per month
    Features:
    • All features of the Security Plan
    • Turn-by-Turn Navigation
    • AtYourService (discounts and offers from merchants)
    • Enhanced Connected Navigation

    The Guidance Plan offers advanced navigation and travel-related features, ideal for frequent travelers or those who rely on detailed driving directions and real-time assistance.


  3. OnStar Premium Plan
    4. Cost: $59.99 per month
    Features:
    • All features of the Guidance Plan
    • Unlimited 4G LTE Wi-Fi hotspot
    • Streaming services for rear-seat passengers
    • Enhanced Infotainment options

    The Premium Plan is the most comprehensive offering, combining safety, security, navigation, and connectivity features, along with unlimited in-car Wi-Fi, making it perfect for tech-savvy users and families on the go.


Add-On Plans

  1. OnStar Family Link
    2. Cost: $3.99 per month
    Features:
    • Location Alerts
    • Vehicle Location Tracking

    This add-on plan is ideal for families wanting to keep track of their loved ones' whereabouts, ensuring peace of mind and safety.


  2. OnStar Data Plans'
    3. Cost: Varies ($15 - $40 per month)
    Features:
    • Data packages for in-car Wi-Fi ranging from 1GB to unlimited data

    OnStar offers several data plans to cater to different internet usage needs, from light browsing to heavy streaming and multiple device connectivity.


How to Subscribe to OnStar?

Subscribing to OnStar is straightforward. You can sign up for a plan through the following methods:

  1. Dealership: When purchasing a new GM vehicle, the dealership can assist in setting up your OnStar subscription.
  2. Online: Visit the OnStar website to explore plans and sign up directly.
  3. OnStar Mobile App: Download the OnStar app, create an account, and select your desired plan.
  4. Phone: Call OnStar customer service to discuss plans and activate your subscription.

Benefits of OnStar

  • Safety and Security: With features like Automatic Crash Response, Emergency Services, and Stolen Vehicle Assistance, OnStar provides peace of mind and immediate help in emergencies.
  • Connectivity: OnStar’s Wi-Fi hotspot and connected services ensure you stay connected on the road, whether for work, entertainment, or navigation.
  • Convenience: Features like remote vehicle management and diagnostics help you maintain your vehicle and address issues promptly.
  • Cost Savings: Plans like AtYourService offer discounts and deals from various merchants, potentially saving you money on everyday expenses.

OnStar offers a variety of plans to cater to different needs, from essential safety features to comprehensive connectivity and infotainment options. Whether you’re looking for basic emergency services or advanced navigation and Wi-Fi capabilities, OnStar has a plan that can fit your lifestyle and budget. By understanding the costs and features of each plan, you can make an informed decision that enhances your driving experience and provides peace of mind on the road.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Can I get OnStar without a GM vehicle?

No, OnStar services are exclusive to GM vehicles. However, GM has expanded some features to non-GM vehicles through its mobile app services, but full integration remains limited to GM models.


2. Is there a contract for OnStar services?

OnStar services are subscription-based, typically on a month-to-month basis. 3. Can I transfer my OnStar subscription to a new vehicle?

Yes, you can transfer your OnStar subscription to another GM vehicle. Contact OnStar customer service for assistance with the transfer process.


4. Are there any discounts available for OnStar plans?

OnStar occasionally offers promotional discounts and deals. Additionally, certain GM vehicle purchases may include free trial periods for OnStar services.


5. How do I cancel my OnStar subscription?

You can cancel your OnStar subscription by contacting OnStar customer service via phone or through the OnStar mobile app. Be sure to have your account details ready for the cancellation process.


6. Is OnStar worth the cost?

The value of OnStar depends on your needs and how much you use the features. For those who prioritize safety, connectivity, and convenience, OnStar can be a valuable addition to their vehicle.
