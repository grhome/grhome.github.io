How to Begin Your Audible Free Trial: A Step-by-Step Guide

Embarking on your Audible Free Trial is straightforward and promises a gateway to countless stories. Follow these simple steps to start your adventure in the land of audiobooks.

Step 1: Visit the Audible Website

Start by navigating to the Audible website. Right on the homepage, you’ll find the offer for a free trial. It’s hard to miss, often highlighted with appealing visuals or banners.

Step 2: Create or Log In to Your Amazon Account

Audible is part of the Amazon family, so you will need an Amazon account to sign up for the Audible Free Trial. If you already have an account, simply log in. If not, take a moment to create one. It’s quick and free.

Step 3: Sign Up for the Free Trial

Once you’re logged in, go back to the free trial page and click on the ‘Start your free trial’ button. You’ll be prompted to choose a subscription plan, but don’t worry, you can cancel anytime during the trial period without any charge.

Step 4: Enter Your Payment Information

Even though it’s a free trial, Audible requires a payment method on file. This is to allow seamless continuation should you choose to keep your subscription after the trial ends. You won’t be charged during the trial period, and you can cancel anytime.

Step 5: Download the App and Enjoy

After setting up your account and entering your payment details, download the Audible app on your smartphone, tablet, or another device. Log in with your Amazon account, and you're all set. Start browsing through an extensive library of audiobooks and pick your first listen.


What Do You Need for an Audible Free Trial?

Before you dive into your Audible Free Trial, make sure you have everything needed for a flawless audiobook experience.

Audible works on virtually any device that can play audio. This includes smartphones, tablets, computers, and even many e-readers. Ensure your device is compatible and has sufficient storage for downloads if you prefer listening offline.

A Good Pair of Headphones

While not a necessity, having a good pair of headphones can greatly enhance your listening experience, allowing you to immerse fully in the auditory world of your book.

Stable Internet Connection

For signing up and downloading your first audiobook, a stable internet connection is essential. Once downloaded, you can listen offline, but initial access requires the internet.
