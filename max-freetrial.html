How to Get Max for free on Amazon Prime?

Max, previously known as HBO Max, offers a treasure trove of content. If you're an Amazon Prime member, you're in luck! Here's how you can get the Max free trial through Amazon Prime:

  1. Log in to your Amazon Prime account.
  2. Go to the Amazon Prime Video Channels section.
  3. Search for the Max channel.
  4. Select the Max channel and click on the “Start your free trial” button.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to sign up for the trial. You'll need to enter your payment information, but don’t worry, you won’t be charged until the trial period ends.
  6. Once signed up, log in to your Max account through the Amazon Prime Video app or directly on the Max website to start exploring all the content available!

Steps to Login to Free Max Account

Now that you've signed up for the Max free trial, here’s how to log in and start watching:

  1. Visit the Max website or open the Max app on your device.
  2. Click on the “Sign In” button.
  3. Enter the email and password you used to sign up for the free trial through Amazon Prime.
  4. Click “Sign In” again to access your account.
  5. Start browsing the vast library of movies, TV shows, and exclusive Max originals!

How to Cancel Your Trial Before It Ends?

If you decide that Max isn't for you or you want to avoid any charges after the trial period, you can easily cancel your free trial before it ends. Here’s how:

  1. Log in to your Amazon Prime account.
  2. Go to the Amazon Prime Video Channels section and find the Max channel.
  3. Click on the “Manage Your Prime Video Channels” option.
  4. Find Max in your list of subscriptions and click “Cancel Channel” or “Cancel Subscription.”
  5. Follow the prompts to confirm your cancellation.
