How to Sign Up for Apple Music's Free Trial?

Eager to start your musical journey? Follow these steps to activate your Apple Music Free Trial:

  1. Open the Apple Music app on your device or visit the Apple Music website.
  2. Click on the 'Try it free' button (available on new subscriptions only).
  3. Choose the subscription plan that suits you best. Apple Music offers individual, family, and student plans.
  4. Sign in with your Apple ID and password, or create a new Apple ID if you don’t already have one.
  5. Confirm your billing information. You won’t be charged until the trial period ends, and you can cancel at any time.
  6. Start exploring the expansive library of Apple Music!
