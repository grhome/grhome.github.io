What is YouTube Premium?

YouTube Premium is an enhanced subscription service offered by YouTube that provides an ad-free experience across all videos on the platform. It also allows users to download videos for offline viewing, play videos in the background on mobile devices, and access all the content on YouTube Music and YouTube Originals. Essentially, it turns your YouTube experience into a more versatile and enjoyable journey.


How to Start Your YouTube Premium Free Trial?

Starting your YouTube Premium free trial is like setting sail on a voyage towards uninterrupted entertainment. Here’s how to embark on this adventure:

  1. Visit the YouTube Premium Page: Navigate to the YouTube Premium section on the YouTube website or app. This page details all the benefits and features of the subscription.
  2. Select the 'Try it Free' Option: Find and click on the "Try it Free" button. This will prompt you to sign into your Google account if you haven't already.
  3. Review the Plan Details: Once signed in, you'll be shown the different subscription plans available. Review these to select the one that best fits your needs.
  4. Enter Billing Information: Enter your payment details. Although this is a free trial, you will need to provide a payment method to activate it. You won't be charged until the free trial period ends.
  5. Confirm and Enjoy: Confirm your subscription details and start enjoying YouTube like never before—without ads, with background play, and with unlimited downloads.

How to Cancel Your YouTube Premium Free Trial?

If you decide that YouTube Premium isn’t for you, cancelling the free trial is straightforward and hassle-free:

  1. Go to Your Account: Click on your profile picture in YouTube, then go to “Paid memberships”.
  2. Manage Membership: Select “Manage Membership” next to YouTube Premium.
  3. Cancel Membership: Follow the prompts to “Cancel Membership”. Your free trial or subscription will cease at the end of the billing period.
