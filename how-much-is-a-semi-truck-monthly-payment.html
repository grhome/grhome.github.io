How Much is a Semi Truck Monthly Payment?

Make your Payment

Hello there, truck enthusiasts and future truck owners! Today, we’re going to dive into the world of semi-trucks. Specifically, we’re going to talk about how much it costs to have one of these beasts in your garage. So, buckle up and let’s hit the road!


Before we get into the nitty-gritty, let’s cover some basics. A semi-truck, also known as a tractor-trailer, is a large vehicle used to transport goods. These trucks are a common sight on highways and are crucial to the economy. But owning one isn’t cheap. There are several costs involved, including the purchase price, maintenance, fuel, and insurance. However, the focus of our discussion today is the monthly payment for a semi-truck.


How Much is a Semi Truck Monthly Payment?

The cost of your monthly semi-truck payment will depend on the terms of your loan. You can use a business loan calculator to estimate how much your monthly payment will be.


Here’s a simple formula used to calculate the monthly payment:

Monthly Payment=1−(1+r)−nr×PV​

In this formula:

  • r is the monthly interest rate (annual rate / 12).
  • PV is the present value or principal of the loan (price - down payment).
  • n is the number of periods or payments.

Leasing vs. Buying

When it comes to acquiring a semi-truck, you have two main options: leasing or buying. Leasing a new truck can cost between $1,600 and $2,500 a month, while leasing used trucks costs about half that much. Leases may also come with mileage limits.


On the other hand, if you’re looking to buy, the average costs to lease a semi-truck range between $800 and $2,500 per month. For a new vehicle, you can expect those cost estimates to average between $1,600 and $2,500. But, if you want a second-hand truck, monthly costs will probably top out at around $1,600 per month.


Wrapping Up

Owning a semi-truck is a significant investment, and understanding the costs involved is crucial. Whether you choose to lease or buy, knowing your monthly payments can help you make an informed decision. Remember, it’s always a good idea to use a loan calculator and consult with a financial advisor before making such a big commitment.
