How Much is Sling TV Per Month?

Explore Plans

Are you considering cutting the cord and wondering if Sling TV is the right choice for your streaming needs? If so, you're in the right place! Sling TV is a popular live TV streaming service that offers a variety of packages and channels to fit different viewing preferences and budgets. But how much does it actually cost per month? Let's dive into the details and explore what Sling TV has to offer.


Sling TV Packages

  1. Sling Orange: This package is great for families and sports enthusiasts. It includes channels like ESPN, Disney Channel, CNN, and HGTV. You get around 32 channels in total.
  2. Sling Blue: This package caters to news and entertainment lovers. It includes channels like Fox, NBC, Bravo, and FX. It offers around 42 channels.
  3. Sling Orange & Blue: For those who don't want to miss out on anything, the combined package includes all the channels from both Sling Orange and Sling Blue, giving you about 50+ channels.

Add-On Packages

Sling TV also offers various add-on packages that allow you to customize your viewing experience further. These include:

  1. Sports Extra: Additional sports channels like NBA TV, NHL Network, and beIN Sports.
  2. Comedy Extra: Channels like MTV, TruTV, and GSN.
  3. Kids Extra: Additional kids' channels like Boomerang, Disney Junior, and Nicktoons.
  4. News Extra: Additional news channels like CNBC, BBC World News, and Newsmax.

Which Sling TV Package is Best?

Choosing the best Sling TV package depends on your viewing preferences and needs. Here's a closer look at each package to help you decide:

Sling Orange:

  • Best for: Sports fans and families with young children.
  • Pros: Access to ESPN and Disney channels.
  • Cons: Fewer total channels compared to Sling Blue.

Sling Blue:

  • Best for: News junkies and entertainment enthusiasts.
  • Pros: More channels and the ability to stream on up to three devices simultaneously.
  • Cons: No ESPN or Disney channels.

Sling Orange & Blue:

  • Best for: Those who want the best of both worlds.
  • Pros: Access to all channels from both packages and more simultaneous streams.
  • Cons: Higher cost compared to individual packages.

  1. Sports Extra: $11 per month

  3. Kids Extra: $6 per month
  4. News Extra: $6 per month

How Much is Sling Freestream Per Month?

Sling Freestream is a free, ad-supported streaming option that Sling TV offers. It provides access to over 200 live channels and thousands of on-demand movies and TV shows without any subscription fee. However, it does include advertisements, which is how the service remains free to users.


Is Sling TV Free with Amazon Prime?

No, Sling TV is not free with Amazon Prime. They are separate services, and subscribing to one does not grant access to the other. However, you can access the Sling TV app on Amazon Fire TV devices, which makes it convenient if you are an Amazon Prime member using a Fire TV stick or Fire TV.


Why Choose Sling TV?

Sling TV is known for its flexibility and affordability. Here are some reasons why you might consider Sling TV:

  1. Affordability: Sling TV's base packages are competitively priced compared to traditional cable and many other streaming services.
  2. Customization: With various add-on packages, you can tailor your channel lineup to fit your preferences without paying for channels you don't watch.
  3. Accessibility: Sling TV is available on a wide range of devices, including smart TVs, streaming devices (like Roku, Amazon Fire TV, and Apple TV), gaming consoles, and mobile devices.
  4. No Contracts: Sling TV offers the flexibility of a month-to-month subscription without long-term commitments, allowing you to cancel anytime.

How to Get Started with Sling TV?

Getting started with Sling TV is straightforward:

  1. Sign Up: Visit the Sling TV website and choose your preferred package.
  2. Customize: Add any extra packages or premium channels you want.
  3. Download the App: Install the Sling TV app on your preferred device.
  4. Log In and Start Watching: Use your credentials to log in and start enjoying your favorite channels.

Conclusion

Sling TV offers a variety of packages and add-ons to cater to different viewing preferences and budgets. Whether you're a sports fan, a news junkie, or just looking for a cost-effective way to watch your favorite shows, Sling TV has an option for you. With packages starting at $40 per month and the flexibility to customize your lineup, it's a compelling choice for cord-cutters. Plus, with the addition of Sling Freestream, you can enjoy a wealth of content without any subscription fee. So, why not give Sling TV a try and see if it's the right fit for your entertainment needs?
