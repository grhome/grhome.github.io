How Much is Hulu Monthly?

Buy Hulu plan

Are you considering subscribing to Hulu but wondering about the monthly cost? Hulu offers a variety of plans tailored to different needs and budgets. In this article, we'll break down the various Hulu subscription tiers, help you understand the differences between them, and answer the big question: How much is Hulu monthly?


What are the three Hulu plans?

Hulu provides three primary subscription plans to cater to a wide range of viewers:

1. Hulu Basic

Hulu Basic is the most affordable option, priced at $7.99 per month. This plan gives you access to Hulu's extensive library of TV shows, movies, and Hulu Originals. However, it does include ads, which can interrupt your viewing experience.

2. Hulu (No Ads)

If you prefer an uninterrupted viewing experience, the Hulu (No Ads) plan might be the better choice for you. At $14.99 per month, this plan offers the same content as Hulu Basic but without the commercials. It's perfect for those who want to binge-watch their favorite shows without interruptions.

3. Hulu + Live TV

For those who want live TV alongside Hulu's on-demand content, Hulu + Live TV is an excellent option. This plan costs $69.99 per month and includes over 75 live channels, access to Hulu's streaming library, and the ability to record live TV with 50 hours of Cloud DVR storage. It's a comprehensive package for cord-cutters who still want to enjoy live television.


Hulu Packages Description

Hulu packages come with a range of features designed to enhance your viewing experience:

  • Hulu Basic: Access to Hulu's streaming library with limited commercials.

  • Hulu (No Ads): Ad-free access to Hulu's streaming library, perfect for uninterrupted viewing.

  • Hulu + Live TV: Combines Hulu's streaming library with live TV channels, Cloud DVR, and an ad-free on-demand experience for an additional cost.

Each plan offers unique benefits, allowing you to choose the one that best fits your viewing preferences and budget.


What is the difference between Hulu Basic and Hulu Premium?

The main difference between Hulu Basic and Hulu Premium (Hulu No Ads) is the presence of advertisements. Hulu Basic includes ads, making it a more budget-friendly option at $7.99 per month. On the other hand, Hulu Premium, priced at $14.99 per month, eliminates ads for a smoother viewing experience. If you're someone who gets easily annoyed by commercials, Hulu Premium is likely worth the extra cost.

Besides the ad experience, both plans offer the same access to Hulu's extensive library of TV shows, movies, and Hulu Originals. Therefore, your choice between Hulu Basic and Hulu Premium primarily depends on your tolerance for ads and your budget.


Additional Features and Add-Ons

Hulu also offers several add-ons that you can include with your subscription to enhance your viewing experience. These include:

  • Enhanced Cloud DVR: For an additional $9.99 per month, increase your Cloud DVR storage to 200 hours and gain the ability to fast-forward through recorded commercials.

  • Unlimited Screens: For $9.99 per month, you can watch Hulu on as many screens as you want simultaneously.

  • Premium Networks: Add HBO Max, Showtime, Cinemax, or Starz to your subscription for an extra fee, giving you access to more movies and original series.

These add-ons can be combined with any Hulu plan, allowing you to customize your subscription to fit your viewing habits and needs.


How to Choose the Right Hulu Plan for You

Choosing the right Hulu plan depends on your viewing preferences and budget. Here are a few questions to consider:

  • Do you mind watching ads? If not, Hulu Basic is the most cost-effective option.
  • Do you want an ad-free experience? Hulu (No Ads) offers uninterrupted viewing for a higher monthly fee.
  • Do you watch a lot of live TV? Hulu + Live TV combines live channels with on-demand content, making it a comprehensive package for cord-cutters.

By answering these questions, you can determine which Hulu plan best suits your needs.


Conclusion

So, how much is Hulu monthly? The cost ranges from $7.99 for Hulu Basic to $69.99 for Hulu + Live TV. Each plan offers unique features and benefits, allowing you to choose the one that best fits your viewing habits and budget. Whether you prefer a budget-friendly option with ads, an ad-free experience, or a combination of live TV and on-demand content, Hulu has a plan for you. Consider your preferences and choose the plan that makes the most sense for your lifestyle.
