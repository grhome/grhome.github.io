How can I cancel my YouTube free trial?

If you wish to cancel your Youtube free trial, go as guided:

  1. First of all, sign in your Youtube Account.
  2. Tap the Profile icon, select 'Purchases and memberships'.
  3. Then, go to Membership section and click on the 'Manage membership'.
  4. Lastly, select the 'Deactivate' option to terminate your YouTube TV free trial plan.
