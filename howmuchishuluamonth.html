How Much is Hulu a Month?

Explore Hulu Plans

Have you ever wondered, How much is Hulu a month? If you're curious about joining the millions of subscribers who enjoy Hulu's diverse content, you're in for a treat. Let's dive into the details and see how much you can expect to pay for Hulu's various plans and add-ons.


Hulu Subscription Costs

Hulu offers several subscription plans to cater to different viewing preferences and budgets. Here's a breakdown of the current pricing:

  • Hulu (With Ads): $7.99 per month
  • Hulu (No Ads): $14.99 per month
  • Hulu + Live TV (With Ads): $69.99 per month
  • Hulu + Live TV (No Ads): $82.99 per month

Hulu Add-Ons

Hulu also offers several add-on options. If you have an eligible Hulu plan, you can add the following subscriptions:

  • ESPN+: for an additional $10.99 per month
  • Disney+ (with ads): for an additional $2 per month. You can also access Hulu through Disney+, which this month launched “Hulu on Disney+” for $9.99 a month with ads, $19.99 without ads.
  • Max (including some HBO content): for an additional $15.99 per month
  • Cinemax: for an additional $9.99 per month
  • Paramount+ with Showtime: for an additional $11.99 per month
  • Starz: for an additional $9.99 per month

Why Choose Hulu?

Now that we've covered the costs, let's explore why Hulu is a popular choice among streaming services. Hulu offers a rich library of TV shows, movies, and exclusive content, catering to a wide range of tastes. Whether you're a fan of binge-watching classic TV series, discovering new indie films, or staying up-to-date with current episodes, Hulu has something for everyone.

Customizing Your Hulu Experience

One of the exciting aspects of Hulu is the ability to customize your viewing experience with various add-ons. Interested in sports? Add ESPN+ to your subscription. Want to enjoy the magic of Disney? The Disney+ add-on makes it possible. Looking for premium content from HBO or Showtime? Hulu has you covered with Max and Paramount+ add-ons.


Hulu Bundles

If you're looking to get the most bang for your buck, consider Hulu bundles. For example, the Disney Bundle, which includes Hulu, Disney+, and ESPN+, offers a comprehensive entertainment package at a discounted rate. This bundle is perfect for families or individuals who want access to a wide variety of content across different genres and platforms.


Is Hulu Worth It?

With its competitive pricing, extensive content library, and customizable add-ons, Hulu offers a compelling value for streaming enthusiasts. Whether you're interested in live TV, on-demand shows, or exclusive content, Hulu has something to offer. Plus, the ability to add premium channels and services means you can tailor your subscription to fit your unique viewing habits.

If you're still on the fence, consider trying Hulu's free trial to explore its offerings before committing to a subscription. With so many options and features, Hulu makes it easy to find a plan that fits your needs and budget.

In conclusion, the question How much is Hulu a month? is just the beginning of the journey. Understanding the different plans, add-ons, and bundles can help you make an informed decision and get the most out of your streaming experience. Happy watching!
