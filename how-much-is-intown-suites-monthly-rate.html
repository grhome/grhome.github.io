How much is intown suites monthly rate?

Book Now

Looking for an extended stay hotel with affordable rates? InTown Suites might be just what you need. With locations across the United States, InTown Suites offers a comfortable, convenient, and budget-friendly option for both short-term and long-term stays. But how much does it cost to stay at InTown Suites for a month? Let’s break down the costs and explore how to make the most of your stay.


Understanding InTown Suites Monthly Rates

The monthly rate at InTown Suites can vary depending on the location, room type, and time of year. On average, you can expect to pay between $1,200 and $1,800 per month. This rate typically includes utilities, cable TV, and weekly housekeeping. For a more precise rate, it’s best to contact the specific InTown Suites location you’re interested in.


What’s Included in the Monthly Rate?

When you stay at InTown Suites, the monthly rate covers several amenities and services, such as:

  • Fully Furnished Rooms: Each suite comes with a bed, seating area, and a kitchenette with a refrigerator and microwave.
  • Utilities: Electricity, water, and gas are included in the rate, so you don’t have to worry about extra bills.
  • Cable TV: Enjoy a variety of channels for entertainment.
  • High-Speed Internet: Stay connected with complimentary WiFi.
  • Weekly Housekeeping: Your room will be cleaned once a week, including fresh linens and towels.
  • On-Site Laundry Facilities: Convenient laundry rooms are available for guests.

How to Book a Monthly Stay at InTown Suites?

Booking a monthly stay at InTown Suites is straightforward. Here’s how you can do it:

Step 1: Choose Your Location

Visit the InTown Suites website and select the location where you want to stay. You can view availability and rates for each property.

Step 2: Select Your Room Type

Different room types are available, including standard suites and premium suites. Choose the one that best fits your needs and budget.

Step 3: Make a Reservation

You can book your stay online or by calling the property directly. Be sure to specify that you’re interested in a monthly rate.

Step 4: Check-In

When you arrive, check in at the front desk. You’ll need to provide a valid ID and payment for your stay.


How to Renew My Stay in InTown Suites?

If you decide to extend your stay beyond the initial month, renewing is easy:

Contact the Front Desk

Let the front desk staff know that you’d like to renew your stay. They’ll check availability and confirm your new rate.

Update Your Payment

Provide payment for the next month’s stay. You can often pay weekly or monthly, depending on your preference.

Enjoy Your Extended Stay

Continue to enjoy the amenities and services offered by InTown Suites. If you have any special requests or need additional services, the front desk staff is there to help.

Benefits of Staying at InTown Suites

Choosing InTown Suites for your extended stay comes with several benefits:

Affordability

Compared to traditional hotels or short-term rentals, InTown Suites offers competitive rates that include utilities and amenities.

Convenience

With fully furnished rooms, you can move in without the hassle of bringing furniture or household items.

Flexibility

InTown Suites offers flexible stay options, allowing you to extend your stay easily if your plans change.

Locations Nationwide

With locations across the country, you’re likely to find an InTown Suites property near your destination.


Conclusion

So, how much is InTown Suites monthly rate? Depending on the location and room type, you can expect to pay between $1,200 and $1,800 per month. This rate includes utilities, cable TV, high-speed internet, and weekly housekeeping, making it a convenient and cost-effective option for extended stays. Whether you’re relocating, traveling for work, or need temporary housing, InTown Suites offers a comfortable and affordable solution.
