Getting Started with the free trial on Apple TV

Ready to test out the waters? Signing up for the Apple TV Free Trial is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Visit the Apple TV+ website or open the Apple TV app on your device.
  2. Click on the 'Start Your Free Trial' button. You'll see this prominently displayed on the main page.
  3. Sign in with your Apple ID and password. If you don’t have an Apple ID, you’ll need to create one first.
  4. Confirm your billing information. While no charges are made upfront, Apple requires a payment method on file for the subscription to continue after the trial period.
  5. Enjoy your free trial! Stream Apple TV+ content directly from your browser or through the Apple TV app on your devices like iPhone, iPad, Mac, or your smart TV.

How to Activate 3 Months Free Apple TV+?

Did you know? You might be eligible for a 3-month free trial of Apple TV+. This special promotion is available with the purchase of a new Apple device. Here’s how you can activate it:

  1. Turn on your new Apple device and sign in with your Apple ID.
  2. Open the Apple TV app on the device.
  3. You should see the offer displayed in the app. If not, head to the Apple TV+ tab where you can activate your offer.
  4. Click "Accept" to begin your three months free.

Troubleshooting: Why Am I Unable to Activate 3 Months trial?

Running into issues? Here’s a quick troubleshooting guide to help you figure out why you might be having trouble activating your 3 months of free Apple TV+:

  • Eligibility: This offer can only be claimed on new Apple devices that were purchased after September 10, 2019, and it must be claimed within 90 days of your device activation.
  • Previous Subscriptions: If you’ve already activated a free trial for Apple TV+ previously, you might not be eligible to claim another free period.
  • Software Update: Ensure that your device is running the latest version of iOS, iPadOS, macOS, or tvOS. Sometimes, a pending update can prevent the offer from appearing.
  • App Update: Check if the Apple TV app is updated to the latest version. Older versions of the app might not show the latest offers.
  • Internet Connection: A stable internet connection is required to activate the offer. Ensure your device is connected to the internet.
