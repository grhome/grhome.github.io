How Much is Shopify a Month?

Pick a Plan

Are you ready to embark on an e-commerce adventure with Shopify but are unsure about the costs involved? Fear not! This article will provide a detailed breakdown of Shopify’s monthly pricing, benefits, and free trial information. Let’s dive in!


Shopify is a leading e-commerce platform that offers a range of plans to meet varying budgets and needs. It’s trusted by millions of businesses worldwide, offering a user-friendly interface designed to help businesses connect with customers, drive sales, and manage operations.


Shopify Monthly Subscription Plans

Shopify offers several pricing plans, each with its own set of features and benefits. Here’s a detailed rundown:

  1. Basic Shopify Plan: Priced at $29 USD/month when billed yearly, this plan is perfect for solo entrepreneurs. It includes features like up to 77% shipping discount, 10 inventory locations, 24/7 chat support, and localized global selling in 3 markets. This plan is a great starting point for those new to e-commerce.

  2. Shopify Plan: At $79 USD/month when billed yearly, this plan is designed for small teams. It offers up to 88% shipping discount and insurance, 5 additional staff accounts, and the same features as the Basic plan. This plan is ideal for businesses looking to grow and expand their online presence.

  3. Advanced Shopify Plan: For businesses that are scaling up, this $299 USD/month plan (billed yearly) could be a good fit. It includes up to 88% shipping discount, insurance, 3rd-party calculated rates, custom reports and analytics, 15 additional staff accounts, and 10x checkout capacity. This plan is designed for businesses that need advanced features and reporting.

  4. Shopify Plus Plan: For more complex businesses, this plan starts at $2,300 USD/month on a 3-year term. It offers up to 88% shipping discount, insurance, 3rd-party calculated rates, custom reports and analytics, 200 inventory locations, priority 24/7 phone support, localized global selling in 50 markets, unlimited staff accounts, and fully customizable checkout with 40x capacity. This plan is designed for large businesses and enterprises with complex needs.

  5. Shopify Starter Plan: This plan costs just $5 USD/month, perfect for those who want to sell instantly through social media and messaging apps or a simple online store. This plan is a cost-effective option for those who are just starting out or have a limited budget.

Shopify Free Trial

Shopify offers a free trial for 3 days, followed by an extension where the first month costs just $1. This is a great opportunity to explore all of Shopify’s features and tools. You can set up your store, add products, and even make some sales before you decide to commit to a plan.


Comparing Shopify Plans

Each Shopify plan is designed to cater to different business needs and budgets. The Basic plan is perfect for solo entrepreneurs starting their journey, while the Shopify plan is ideal for small teams. The Advanced and Plus plans offer more features and are suited for larger, more complex businesses. The Starter plan is a cost-effective option for those who want to sell through social media and messaging apps.


In conclusion, Shopify offers a range of pricing plans to suit different business needs. With its free trial and $1 first-month offer, you can explore the platform and find the plan that best fits your business. So, are you ready to bring your e-commerce dreams to life with Shopify?
