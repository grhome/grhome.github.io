How Much is Shopify a Month?

Advertisement

When it comes to launching an online store, Shopify stands out as one of the leading e-commerce platforms. But how much does it actually cost to use Shopify on a monthly basis? In this article, we will break down the various plans, pricing tiers, and the benefits each plan offers to help you decide which one is best for your business.


Shopify’s Pricing Structure

Shopify offers several pricing plans tailored to meet the needs of different business sizes and stages. Here's an overview of the main plans:

  • Shopify Starter: $5 per month
  • Basic Shopify: $39 per month
  • Shopify: $105 per month
  • Advanced Shopify: $399 per month
  • Shopify Plus: Custom pricing starting at $2000 per month

Each plan offers a unique set of features designed to support businesses at various stages of growth. Let's delve into each plan in detail.


Shopify Starter

  • Price: $5 per month

The Shopify Starter plan is designed for those who are just starting out and want to sell products without the need for a full-fledged online store. This plan is perfect for those who want to leverage social media platforms or existing websites to sell their products.


Key Features:

  • Access to the Shopify Buy Button
  • Integration with social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram
  • Basic order and product management tools
  • Mobile app for managing your store on the go

Benefits:

  • Extremely affordable entry point
  • Ideal for testing the waters before committing to a higher plan
  • Seamless integration with social media, allowing for a wider audience reach

Basic Shopify: The Essential Toolkit

  • Price: $39 per month

The Basic Shopify plan is suitable for new e-commerce businesses that need more than just a Buy Button. This plan offers a complete online store with all the essential features required to manage and grow an online business.


Key Features:

  • Full online store and blog
  • Unlimited products
  • Two staff accounts
  • 24/7 support
  • Discount codes
  • Manual order creation

Benefits:

  • Comprehensive tools to set up and manage an online store
  • 24/7 support ensures help is always available
  • Ability to create discount codes to attract more customers
  • More control over your store’s design and functionality

Shopify: Scaling Up

  • Price: $105 per month

The Shopify plan is designed for growing businesses that require more advanced features and reporting capabilities. It strikes a balance between affordability and enhanced functionality.

Key Features:

  • Everything in Basic Shopify, plus:
  • Five staff accounts
  • Professional reports
  • Standard POS (Point of Sale) features
  • Lower credit card rates

Benefits:

  • Professional reporting helps make informed business decisions
  • Lower transaction fees save money on every sale
  • More staff accounts allow for better team management
  • Enhanced POS features for businesses with physical locations

Advanced Shopify: For the Expanding Enterprise

  • Price: $399 per month

Advanced Shopify is aimed at larger businesses with significant sales volumes and more complex needs. This plan offers advanced features that support extensive business operations and provide in-depth analytics.

Key Features:

  • Advanced reporting tools for detailed insights and custom reports
  • Third-party calculated shipping rates provide accurate shipping costs to customers
  • Additional staff accounts support larger teams
  • Significant savings on transaction fees for high-volume sales

Shopify Plus: Enterprise-Level Power

  • Price: Starting at $2000 per month (custom pricing)

Shopify Plus is the enterprise solution for high-volume merchants and large businesses requiring advanced features, dedicated support, and the ability to scale seamlessly.


Key Features:

  • Customizable checkout
  • Dedicated account manager
  • High-level security and compliance
  • API access for custom integrations
  • Multiple store management

Benefits:

  • Customizable checkout allows for a tailored customer experience
  • Dedicated account manager ensures personalized support
  • Enhanced security features protect customer data and transactions
  • Ability to manage multiple stores from a single account
  • Access to advanced APIs for bespoke solutions and integrations

Additional Costs

While the monthly subscription fee is a primary consideration, it's important to account for other potential costs:

  • Transaction Fees: Shopify charges a transaction fee if you do not use Shopify Payments. These fees vary by plan.
  • Credit Card Rates: Rates for using Shopify Payments also vary by plan, typically decreasing with higher-tier plans.
  • Apps and Plugins: Many businesses use additional apps from the Shopify App Store to enhance their store's functionality. Some of these apps come with their own monthly fees.
  • Themes: While Shopify offers free themes, there are also paid themes available that offer more customization options and features.

Conclusion

Choosing the right Shopify plan depends on your business needs, size, and growth expectations. Whether you're a budding entrepreneur just starting out or a large enterprise looking to scale, Shopify offers a plan that can support your e-commerce journey. From the affordability of the Starter plan to the robust features of Shopify Plus, there is a solution tailored for every stage of business growth.


By understanding the costs and benefits of each plan, you can make an informed decision that aligns with your business goals and budget.
