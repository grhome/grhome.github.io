Netflix Subscription Plans and Costs

Netflix offers a variety of subscription plans to cater to different viewing habits and budgets12. Here’s a detailed breakdown:

  1. Standard with Ads: This is the most affordable plan at $6.99 per month1. It includes ad-supported content, unlimited mobile games, and allows you to watch on 2 supported devices at a time in Full HD.

  2. Standard: Priced at $15.49 per month1, this plan offers unlimited ad-free movies, TV shows, and mobile games. You can watch on 2 supported devices at a time in Full HD1. An extra member who doesn’t live with you can be added for an additional $7.99 per month.

  3. Premium: This plan costs $22.99 per month1 and offers unlimited ad-free movies, TV shows, and mobile games. You can watch on 4 supported devices at a time in Ultra HD. Up to 2 extra members who don’t live with you can be added for an additional $7.99 each per month.

How to Get Netflix for Free?

While Netflix doesn’t offer a completely free plan, there are ways to enjoy its services without a monthly fee. Many new Netflix users get a 30-day free trial. During this period, you can enjoy all the features of the chosen plan for free. However, you need to provide payment details to start the free trial and you will be automatically charged once the trial period ends unless you cancel it.


In conclusion, Netflix offers a range of plans to suit different needs and budgets. Whether you’re into movies, series, or documentaries, Netflix has got you covered. And with the possibility of getting the service for free for a month, it’s definitely worth checking out!
