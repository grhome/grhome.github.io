Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What happens after the free trial ends?

Once your Walmart Plus free trial concludes, your subscription will automatically convert into a paid membership. If you decide it's not for you, remember to cancel before the trial ends to avoid any charges.

Can I cancel the free trial?

Absolutely! You can cancel the Walmart Plus free trial at any time during the trial period without penalty. This allows you to experience the full benefits without the worry of being locked in.

Are there any restrictions during the free trial?

No, there aren't any restrictions. You will enjoy all the benefits that a paid subscriber receives, which includes access to all special discounts and services.

Do I need to enter my credit card details to sign up?

Yes, signing up for the Walmart Plus free trial requires a valid credit card or debit card. You will not be charged during the free trial period, and this information is needed to facilitate a smooth transition to a paid membership if you choose to continue.
