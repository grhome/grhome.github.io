Have you ever wondered, How much is Peacock a month? If you're curious about joining the millions of subscribers who enjoy Peacock's diverse content, you're in the right place. Let's dive into the details and see how much you can expect to pay for Peacock's various plans and add-ons.

Peacock offers two main subscription plans to cater to different viewing preferences and budgets. Here's a breakdown of the current pricing:

Plan Cost Features
Peacock Premium $5.99 per month
  • Access to all Peacock originals

Peacock Premium Plus $11.99 per month
