Disney Plus Free Trial

Start Free Trial on Disney Plus

Hey there, streaming enthusiast! Are you looking for a way to dive into the magical world of Disney without spending a dime? You’ve probably heard about Disney Plus, the streaming service that brings you everything from classic Disney movies to Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar, and National Geographic content. But the big question is: Can you get Disney Plus for free? Stick around as we unravel this question and explore some exciting twists and turns.


Can I Get Disney Plus for Free?

Alright, let's get straight to it. Can you get Disney Plus for free? The answer is both yes and no. Here’s the deal:

Disney Plus used to offer a 7-day free trial when it first launched, allowing users to explore its vast library without paying. However, this offer has since been discontinued. But don’t lose hope! There are still ways to enjoy Disney Plus without reaching for your wallet immediately.


Special Promotions and Bundles

One way to get Disney Plus for free is through special promotions and bundles. Sometimes, telecom companies and other service providers offer Disney Plus as part of their packages. For example:

  • Verizon Wireless: In the past, Verizon has offered its customers a free Disney Plus subscription for up to a year with certain unlimited plans.
  • Hulu and ESPN+ Bundle: You can subscribe to the Disney Bundle, which includes Disney Plus, Hulu, and ESPN+ at a discounted rate. Sometimes, this bundle is offered as part of promotional deals.

Free with Rewards Programs

Another way to snag Disney Plus for free is through rewards programs. Some credit cards and loyalty programs offer Disney Plus subscriptions as part of their rewards. Check with your credit card provider or loyalty program to see if they offer any such deals.


Sharing is Caring

Have a friend or family member who already has a Disney Plus subscription? You might be in luck! Disney Plus allows up to four simultaneous streams per account. If you know someone who’s willing to share their account, you can enjoy Disney Plus for free.


Is Disney+ Free on Amazon Prime?

This is another popular question. Amazon Prime offers a plethora of benefits, including Prime Video, which has a vast collection of movies and TV shows. But is Disney+ free on Amazon Prime? The short answer is no.

Disney Plus and Amazon Prime are separate streaming services. While Amazon Prime offers a lot of great content, you’ll need a separate subscription for Disney Plus. However, you can access Disney Plus on Amazon Fire TV devices, making it easy to stream your favorite Disney content if you already have a Fire Stick or Fire TV.


How to Get Disney Plus on Amazon Fire TV?

If you have an Amazon Fire TV device, here’s how you can get Disney Plus on it:

  1. Turn on your Amazon Fire TV device and go to the home screen.
  2. Use the search function to look for the Disney Plus app.
  3. Select the app and click on “Download” or “Get”.
  4. Once the app is installed, open it and log in with your Disney Plus credentials.
  5. Start streaming your favorite Disney movies and shows!

Why Choose Disney Plus?

Now that we’ve covered how to potentially get Disney Plus for free and the specifics about Amazon Prime, let’s talk about why you might want to subscribe to Disney Plus in the first place. Here are some compelling reasons:

  • Exclusive Content: Disney Plus is home to a vast library of exclusive content, including beloved Disney classics, Pixar animations, Marvel superhero movies, Star Wars sagas, and National Geographic documentaries.
  • Original Series: Disney Plus features original series that you can’t find anywhere else, such as "The Mandalorian," "WandaVision," and "Loki."
  • Ad-Free Experience: Enjoy an uninterrupted, ad-free streaming experience with Disney Plus.
  • Family-Friendly: Disney Plus offers a wealth of family-friendly content, making it a great choice for households with kids.
  • Affordable Bundles: With the Disney Bundle, you can get Disney Plus, Hulu, and ESPN+ at a discounted rate, providing great value for your money.

Conclusion

While Disney Plus no longer offers a traditional free trial, there are still ways to enjoy this fantastic streaming service without paying upfront. From special promotions to bundles and rewards programs, you have several options to explore. Although Disney+ isn’t included with Amazon Prime, it’s easy to access on Amazon Fire TV devices, ensuring you can enjoy your favorite content with ease.

So, whether you’re a Marvel fanatic, a Star Wars junkie, or just love a good Disney classic, Disney Plus has something for everyone. Keep an eye out for promotions and make the most of any opportunities to enjoy Disney Plus for free. Happy streaming!
