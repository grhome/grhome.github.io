How Much is Onstar a Month?

View Plan

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to have a guardian angel in your car, looking out for you every time you hit the road? Well, that’s exactly what OnStar aims to provide. But how much does this peace of mind cost? Let’s find out.


OnStar is a subsidiary of General Motors that provides a variety of services such as in-vehicle security, hands-free calling, turn-by-turn navigation, and remote diagnostics systems. Available throughout the United States, Canada, and many other countries, OnStar offers a range of subscription plans to suit different needs and budgets.


OnStar Subscription Plans

OnStar offers several subscription plans, each with its own set of features and benefits. Here’s a simple breakdown of the monthly costs for each plan:

  • Premium Plan - $49.99/month: This plan offers all the safety, convenience, and features that OnStar has to offer. It includes Automatic Crash Response, Emergency Services, Stolen Vehicle Assistance, Roadside Assistance, OnStar Guardian app, Remote Vehicle Access, Unlimited In-Vehicle Data, and In-Vehicle Apps.

  • Preferred Plus Wi-Fi Plan - $44.99/month: This plan includes all the features of the Premium plan, but at a slightly lower cost.

  • Essentials Plan - $39.99/month: This plan offers OnStar safety combined with some amazing conveniences. It includes Automatic Crash Response, Emergency Services, Stolen Vehicle Assistance, Roadside Assistance, OnStar Guardian app, Remote Vehicle Access, Unlimited In-Vehicle Data, and In-Vehicle Apps.

  • Preferred Plan - $29.99/month: This plan offers the same features as the Essentials plan but at a lower cost.

  • Safety & Security Plan - $29.99/month: This plan focuses on safety and security, offering features like Automatic Crash Response, Roadside Assistance, and the OnStar Guardian app.

  • Connected Vehicle Plan - $24.99/month: This plan turns your car into a convenient, connected marvel. It offers features like Wi-Fi Hotspot, In-Vehicle Apps, vehicle mobile app, and more.

  • Unlimited Data Plan - $25/month: play with fast, reliable Wi-Fi.

Is There a Free OnStar Plan?

While OnStar doesn’t currently offer a completely free plan, they do offer several trial periods at no cost. Many new GM vehicles come with complimentary OnStar services for 30-day trial periods. However, after the trial period ends, you’ll need to choose a paid plan to continue using OnStar services.


Does OnStar Still Work Without Subscription?

Unfortunately, most OnStar services are not available without an active subscription. Once your subscription expires, your vehicle sends out a signal to OnStar to disconnect virtually all services. However, some features may still be available through smartphone projection with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.

In conclusion, while OnStar does come with a cost, the peace of mind it provides with its safety and security features can be invaluable. Whether you’re looking for basic safety features or a comprehensive package, there’s an OnStar plan to fit your needs and budget.
