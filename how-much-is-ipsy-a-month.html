How Much Is IPSY a Month?

Shop Now

IPSY is a beauty subscription service that brings personalized beauty products straight to your doorstep. Whether you’re a makeup enthusiast or just dipping your toes into the beauty world, IPSY offers a delightful surprise each month. Here’s how it works:

  1. Beauty Quiz: It all begins with a Beauty Quiz. You answer questions about your beauty preferences, skin tone, hair color, eye color, and favorite brands. This information helps IPSY curate products unique to you.

  2. Monthly Assortment: Based on your quiz responses, IPSY delivers a new assortment of beauty goodies each month. These products range from liquid lipsticks to eye creams, nail polishes, and face masks. There’s something for everyone!

  3. Personalization: What sets IPSY apart is its personalization. You have a say in what goes into your bag. You can tweak your preferences, choose additional products, and make it truly yours.

  4. Billing and Shipping: For your first bag, you’re billed as soon as you join. After that, billing occurs on the last day of the month for the next month’s bag. Your bag ships out about two weeks later.


How Much Does IPSY Cost?

IPSY offers different membership plans to suit various beauty needs. Let’s break them down:

  1. Glam Bag: Starting at just $14/month, the Glam Bag is the OG subscription. It includes five personalized, deluxe-size beauty samples (worth up to $70) sent in an adorable makeup bag. Plus, you get to choose one product for your bag each month. It’s no wonder this is IPSY’s most popular membership!

  2. BoxyCharm: If you’re all about full-size products, consider BoxyCharm. Priced at $30/month, it delivers five personalized, full-size beauty products (worth up to $200). It’s like unwrapping a beauty treasure chest!

  3. Ultimate Glam Bag: For those who want it all, the Ultimate Glam Bag costs $44/month and packs a punch. You’ll receive a gorgeous Glam Bag with 5+ products (up to $270 in value), exclusive offers, and how-to video tutorials from IPSY Stylists. It’s the ultimate beauty indulgence!


Is IPSY Worth It?

Now, let’s address the million-dollar question: Is IPSY worth it?

  • Variety: IPSY introduces you to a wide range of products. It’s like having a beauty playground delivered to your doorstep. You get to try different brands, shades, and formulas.

  • Discovery: IPSY encourages exploration. Maybe you’ll discover a holy grail product you wouldn’t have picked up otherwise. It’s like having a personal beauty curator.

  • Cost-Effective: Considering the value of the products you receive, IPSY is a steal. Even the Glam Bag at $14/month offers substantial value.

  • Fun Surprise: Who doesn’t love a monthly surprise? Unboxing your IPSY bag feels like a mini celebration.

However, remember that beauty preferences are subjective. Some people adore IPSY, while others prefer buying full-size products they’ve researched extensively. It ultimately depends on your beauty journey and what brings you joy.


So, if you’re ready for a delightful monthly treat, IPSY is definitely worth considering. Your unique beauty deserves to shine!
